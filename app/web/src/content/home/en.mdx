# Welcome to Kol Torah

Kol Torah will be a unique website that aggregates Torah lessons from various rabbis and enables advanced AI-based searches within their content.

The site was preceded by an AI usage experiment, which resulted in the creation of a site that aggregates all of 
<a href="https://v0-butbul-halacha-ui.vercel.app/?page=1" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rabbi Aharon Butbul's daily Halachot</a>.

The Kol Torah website will be built by volunteers.
